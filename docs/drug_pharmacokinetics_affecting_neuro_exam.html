1 Drug Pharmacokinetics Affecting Neuro Exam

Common Agents Affecting Neurologic Exam

Barbiturates Suggested time to brain death exam Distribution Half Life (t½) Drug

Pentobarbital level ≤ 10 mcg/ml ~35-45% bound to plasma proteins ~4-50 hrs Pentobarbital

Phenobarbital level ≤ 10 mcg/ml ~20-45% bound to plasma proteins ~2-6 days Phenobarbital

Benzodiazepines Suggested time to brain death exam Distribution Half Life (t½) Drug

Variable, if expect toxicity, wait 1.5-2 times the half life

Widely in most body tissues 10-20 hrs Lorazepam Widely, 95% protein bound 1-12.5 hrs Midazolam

Widely, 94-99% protein bound 20-50 hrs, active metabolite 40-100 hrs Diazepam

Widely 10-48 hrs, active metabolite 14-95 hrs Chlordiazepoxide

Neuromuscular blocking agents Suggested time to brain death exam Distribution Half Life (t½) Drug

Train of four via peripheral nerve stimulation should result in four thumb twitches

Low body distribution Biphasic t½ (2-3.4 min and 20 min respectively) Atracurium

87% protein bound Triphasic t½, terminal t½ ~2hr Pancuronium Rocuronium

Widely Metabolized rapidly, with effect lasting ~10 min Succinylcholine

Not fully characterized Biphasic t½ (3-9 min and 30-80 min respectively) Vecuronium

Narcotics Suggested time to brain death exam Distribution Half Life (t½) Drug

Variable, if expect toxicity, wait 1.5-2 times the half life

Widely, 30-35% protein bound 1.5-4.5 hrs Morphine

Widely, 80-86% protein bound 3-7 hrs, duration 30-60 mins Fentanyl

Two distributive t½, 1.27 and 14.7 hrs respectively 2.5 hrs, duration 4-5 hrs Hydromorphone

Widely, 65-80% protein bound 3.2-3.7 hrs, active metabolite 24- 48 hrs Meperidine

N/A N/A N/A Oxycodone

Pupil effects after IV are transient Duration of cardiac effects ~5 mins Atropine
